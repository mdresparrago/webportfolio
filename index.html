Traspecs: Trade Specialist App

The TraSpecs App utilizes Branch-Lock and Offline-Ready Sync to ensure verified, uninterrupted data capture in signal "dead spots" while maintaining strict partner compliance through a Direct-Camera Capture Protocol. The app enables trade specialists to capture and upload receipt data in real-time, even in areas with limited connectivity, ensuring that all transactions are accurately recorded and securely stored. It has a user-friendly interface that allows trade specialists to easily capture and upload receipt data, while also providing managers with real-time insights into SKU-level performance and a centralized module for resolving field concerns plus the offline-ready sync feature ensures that all data is securely stored and synchronized once connectivity is restored, providing a seamless experience for both trade specialists and managers.

This system transforms restricted physical receipts into a secure Digital Item Ledger, providing managers with real-time SKU-level insights and a centralized module for resolving field concerns.

Wertify : Employee Verification Portal

To address the problem with false employee and others who take advantage of the company's identity, the Wertify makes it possible for 3rd party companies to verify the identity and employment status of a person by scanning the QR code imprinted at the back of their ID. The system uses a secure API to connect with the company's database, allowing for real-time verification while ensuring data privacy and security.

In wertify, the QR code serves as a unique identifier that links to the individual's employment records, enabling quick and efficient verification. This solution not only enhances security but also streamlines the verification process for both employees and third-party companies, reducing the risk of identity fraud and ensuring that only authorized personnel can access certain services or benefits. Users can provide feedback about the employee and at the same time can report to HR concerning the employee's behavior.
